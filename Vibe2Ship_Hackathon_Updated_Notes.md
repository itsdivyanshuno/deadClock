## Core Agentic Features

### AI Tools

1. **Task Breakdown Agent**
   - Converts large goals into smaller actionable tasks.

2. **Smart Prioritization Agent**
   - Ranks tasks based on urgency, importance, and effort.

3. **AI Scheduler**
   - Suggests ideal time slots for task completion.

4. **Context-Aware Reminder Agent**
   - Sends reminders based on deadlines and workload.

5. **Habit & Goal Tracker**
